TOPIC:
SEMANTIC HTML

DEFINITION:

Semantic tags clearly describe the purpose of content.

They make code more readable,
SEO friendly and accessible.

TAGS:

<header>

Meaning:
Top section of webpage.

Use:
Logo, title, navigation.

--------------------------------

<nav>

Meaning:
Navigation Section.

Use:
Menus and links.

--------------------------------

<main>

Meaning:
Main content area.

Use:
Stores primary content.

--------------------------------

<section>

Meaning:
Content section.

Use:
Divide webpage into sections.

--------------------------------

<article>

Meaning:
Independent content.

Use:
Blog posts, news articles.

--------------------------------

<aside>

Meaning:
Sidebar content.

Use:
Related links, ads, widgets.

--------------------------------

<footer>

Meaning:
Bottom section.

Use:
Copyright, contact info.
